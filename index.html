Welcome to FocusLab

By Alex & Nari

What is FocusLab?

FocusLab is an educational website designed to help students, professionals, and lifelong learners understand how attention works in everyday life. Many people think focus is simply a matter of “trying harder,” but research in psychology shows that attention is shaped by design choices, habits, and the environments we work in.

Rather than offering generic productivity tips, FocusLab connects ideas from cognitive science to real-world study practices, digital behaviors, and personal routines. Our goal is to help visitors build realistic, sustainable focus habits instead of short bursts of motivation.

What You Can Find Here

FocusLab is organized into a small set of pages that you can explore from the menu in the upper-right corner. Each page focuses on a different part of the overall goal: helping people understand their attention and build more sustainable study habits.

The About page introduces the project, explains why we created FocusLab. The Tools page collects simple exercises, such as focus timers, distraction checklists, and reflection prompts that visitors can try in their own study routines. The Resources page gathers articles, guides, and other media for people who want to read more about attention, multitasking, and learning strategies. The Home page, where you are now, gives an overview of the site and suggests how to get started.

How to Navigate

Use the navigation bar in the upper-right corner to move between sections. The Resources page gathers links for further reading, and the About page introduces our team and our roles on the project.
